Baursaki Recipe

Description

A traditional flour product of the Altaians, Nogais, Khakassians, Bashkirs, Buryats, Don Cossacks, Kazakhs, Kalmyks, Kyrgyz, Mongols, Tajiks, Tatars, Chuvash, Tuvinians, Turkmen, Uzbeks, Kumyks and Uyghurs.

As a rule, this flour product is prepared from unleavened or yeast dough in the form of small donuts (diamond-shaped or round), made by deep-frying in a cauldron.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 700g
  • Dry yeast - 10 pinches
  • Milk - 1 glass
  • Water - 1 glass
  • Vegetable oil - 50g
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoon

Cooking Proccess

  1. Activate the yeast
  2. Mix milk with sugar
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Send the dough for proofing
  5. Roll out the dough
  6. Make preparations
  7. Fry the baursaks
